# Marathon Training Nutrition Plan

## Daily Macro Framework
- **Carbs**: 55-60% of calories (prioritize for endurance)
- **Protein**: 1.2-1.6g per kg = 82-109g daily
- **Fat**: 20-25% of calories

## Pre-Run (90-min morning run)
**1-2 hours before:**
- 1-2 slices whole grain toast + 1 tbsp peanut butter alternative (tahini) + banana
- Or: Oatmeal with berries and honey
- Small amount of fluid

**During run (if >75 min):**
- Energy gels or sports drink (6-8% carbs)
- Water every 15-20 min

## Post-Run Recovery
**Within 30-60 minutes:**
- 3:1 or 4:1 carb-to-protein ratio
- Example: Greek yogurt with granola + berries, or chickpea pasta with tomato sauce

## Daily Meals

**Breakfast (non-run days):** Oatmeal, eggs, whole grain toast, fruit

**Lunch:** Lentil soup, quinoa bowl, vegetable stir-fry with tofu

**Dinner:** Beans/legumes, whole grains, roasted vegetables, dairy/eggs

**Snacks:** Dried fruit, seeds (sunflower/pumpkin), hummus, whole grain crackers, nut-free alternatives

## Key Tips
✓ Hydrate consistently (aim for light urine color)
✓ Increase calories slightly on long-run days
✓ Include iron-rich foods (lentils, spinach, fortified grains)
✓ Experiment with fueling during training, not race day
